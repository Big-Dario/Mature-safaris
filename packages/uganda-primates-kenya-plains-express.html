Uganda Primates & Kenya Plains Express

Chimpanzee tracking in Kibale's rainforest and a boat safari on the Kazinga Channel, followed by a fast-paced finale among the Masai Mara's big cats.

Chimpanzee TrekkingKazinga Channel Boat SafariQueen Elizabeth NPMasai Mara Big Cats

Itinerary

1
Entebbe → Kibale
Western Uganda rainforest and primate-rich landscape, home to chimpanzees and multiple monkey species. Evening briefing ahead of an early chimp-tracking start.
2
Chimpanzee Trekking in Kibale
Primary rainforest hosts chimpanzee family troops alongside other primates and forest birds. Full-day chimp tracking with experienced local guides.
3
Transfer to Queen Elizabeth NP
Savannah-forest mosaic around the Kazinga Channel, with hippos, elephants and prolific birdlife. Afternoon game drive and sunset over the crater fields.
4
Kazinga Channel Boat Trip & Game Drive
An iconic boat safari past dense hippo pods, crocodiles and prolific waterbirds, followed by an afternoon plains game drive.
5
Fly to Nairobi & Transfer to Masai Mara
A travel day crossing into Kenya, with evening arrival in the Mara and an introductory game drive.
6
Masai Mara Full Day
Open plains and riverine strips hold big cats and large herbivore herds. Full-day drives focus on predator behaviour and seasonal migration hotspots.
7
Masai Mara Morning Drive → Return to Nairobi
Final morning drives and checklist consolidation before the transfer back to Nairobi.
8
Departure from Nairobi
Airport transfer and final arrangements to conclude the primates-and-plains journey.
