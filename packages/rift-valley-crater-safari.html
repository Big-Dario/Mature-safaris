Rift Valley & Crater Safari

Kenya's flamingo-lined Lake Nakuru and the Masai Mara connect through to Tanzania's Lake Manyara and the unmatched Ngorongoro Crater.

Lake Nakuru FlamingosMasai Mara PredatorsLake Manyara Tree-Climbing LionsNgorongoro Crater

Itinerary

1
Nairobi → Lake Nakuru
A shallow alkaline lake with reedbeds and escarpment perches holds seasonal flamingo flocks, pelicans and resident black rhino. Lakeshore dawn watches and raptor scanning.
2
Nakuru Full Day
A lakeshore loop and raptor viewpoints on the escarpment round out exploration of Nakuru's varied habitats.
3
Nakuru → Masai Mara Transfer
Transfer to the Mara with an evening game drive on arrival, entering predator-rich savanna country.
4
Masai Mara Full Day
A full day of predator and plains exploration across the Mara's riverine corridors and open grassland.
5
Mara → Lake Manyara Transfer
A transition to lake-edge habitats and forest drives, crossing into Tanzania toward Lake Manyara.
6
Lake Manyara Full Day
Groundwater forest and alkaline lake hold forest mammals, seasonal flamingos and possible tree-climbing lions. Forest-edge drives and lakeshore exploration.
7
Manyara → Ngorongoro Rim Transfer
An ascent to the highlands ending with sunset at the crater rim.
8
Ngorongoro Crater Drive & Departure
A final crater drive through the caldera's dense wildlife before transfer to Arusha/Kilimanjaro.
