Luxury Fly-in Gorilla & Mara

The fastest, most comfortable way to see both icons — private-guided game drives in an exclusive Mara concession, then a chartered flight straight to gorilla trekking in Bwindi.

Private Mara ConcessionChartered FlightsMountain Gorilla TrekkingMinimal Road Time

Itinerary

1
Fly into Masai Mara (Luxury Camp Arrival)
Arrival at a private conservancy or premium Mara concession with exclusive access. Afternoon game drive targeting high-density predator zones.
2
Mara Private-Guided Drives
Priority morning and evening drives with a private vehicle and guide for tailored, uncrowded sightings.
3
Morning Mara Drive & Charter to Entebbe/Bwindi Region
A final morning drive followed by a comfortable air transfer that minimises road time and maximises rest.
4
Arrival in Bwindi Luxury Lodge & Pre-Trek Briefing
Relaxation and conservation talks at a top-tier forest lodge ahead of the following day's trek.
5
Gorilla Trekking and Luxury Lodge Recovery
A morning gorilla trek followed by spa and leisure time at the lodge.
6
Optional Community Visit or Nature Walks
Gentle activities and curated experiences in the forest-edge community.
7
Charter Back to Entebbe/Nairobi and Departure
Final charter transfers and international connections conclude the ultra-luxury fly-in journey.
