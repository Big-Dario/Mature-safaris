East Africa Classic Safari

The definitive two-country journey — Masai Mara's big cats, the vast Serengeti plains, the wildlife amphitheatre of Ngorongoro Crater, and Lake Manyara's forest-and-lake contrast.

Masai Mara PredatorsSerengeti Migration CountryNgorongoro Crater RhinoLake Manyara Tree-Climbing Lions

Itinerary

1
Nairobi → Masai Mara
Rolling farmland gives way to expansive Mara plains and riverine acacia corridors. First arrivals of zebra, gazelle and resident lion prides. Afternoon orientation drive and dusk camp brief.
2
Masai Mara Full Day
Open grasslands with kopjes and seasonal wetlands host lions, cheetahs and leopards at peak activity. Full-day drives with picnic stops and an optional Maasai cultural visit.
3
Mara → Northern Serengeti
Mara plains continue into the Serengeti's broad grasslands and shallow valleys. Seasonal migration herds and hippos in seasonal pools. Remote-camp afternoon drive and night-sky viewing.
4
Northern Serengeti Full Day
Vast plains with acacia patches and rocky kopjes hold dense wildebeest and zebra concentrations in season, plus resident lion and hyena packs. Dawn drives to river corridors and kopje vantage points.
5
Central Serengeti
Rolling grasslands dotted with kopjes — classic predator country with consistent lion and leopard activity and abundant plains herbivores. Long drives with photographic hides.
6
Serengeti → Ngorongoro Highlands
A climb from open plains into montane grassland and acacia woodland, with transitional herds and raptor watches along the escarpment. Sunset crater-rim viewpoints.
7
Ngorongoro Crater Full Day
A spectacular caldera floor of grassland, woodland and swamp holds high densities of wildebeest, zebra, buffalo and resident black rhino. Full-day crater descent with picnic lunch.
8
Ngorongoro → Lake Manyara
A shift to groundwater forest, cliff escarpments and an alkaline lake, with possible tree-climbing lions and seasonal waterbird flocks. Afternoon drive with riverside and lakeshore walks.
9
Lake Manyara → Arusha Region
Rift-valley farmland and forest-edge patches lead toward Arusha, with local wetland species along the way. Relaxed morning then transfer for market or cultural visit options.
10
Departure from Arusha/Kilimanjaro
Transfer to the airport with a short morning wrap-up and optional marketplace visit if time allows.
