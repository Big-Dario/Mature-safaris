Great Migration + Bwindi Gorilla Adventure

Kenya's Great Migration river crossings meet Uganda's mountain gorillas in one flagship eleven-day expedition — the two most extraordinary wildlife encounters in East Africa.

Great Migration River CrossingsOptional Balloon SafariMountain Gorilla TrekkingBatwa Community Visit

Itinerary

1-4
Masai Mara Migration Focus
Riverine and plains mosaic with crossing points host migration herds and concentrated predators. Intensive river-crossing stakeouts and full-day drives, with an optional balloon safari.
5
Mara → Nairobi → Fly Entebbe
A transit day with evening arrival in Entebbe and onward transfer toward the Bwindi region.
6
Transfer into Bwindi Highlands
Albertine Rift montane forest country. A scenic drive into forest terrain followed by a pre-trek briefing.
7
Gorilla Trekking in Bwindi
A permit-led gorilla trek with a full hour of viewing and photography among the mountain gorilla families.
8
Forest Activities and Community Visits
Gentle forest walks, a Batwa cultural visit, and an optional gorilla habituation experience where available.
9
Return to Entebbe/Kigali with Stops
A leisurely return journey with opportunities for local markets or conservation-project visits.
10
Buffer / Relaxation Day in Entebbe
Botanical garden visits and lakeshore relaxation before departure.
11
Departure
Airport transfer for international flights, concluding the flagship migration-and-gorilla expedition.
