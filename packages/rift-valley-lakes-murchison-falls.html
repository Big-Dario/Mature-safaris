Rift Valley Lakes & Murchison Falls

Kenya's flamingo-lined Rift Valley lakes give way to Uganda's Murchison Falls, where the Nile thunders through a narrow gorge amid elephants, lions and giraffe.

Lake Nakuru FlamingosLake Naivasha Boat RideMurchison Falls Nile CruiseOptional Budongo Chimps

Itinerary

1
Nairobi → Lake Nakuru
Alkaline lake and escarpment woodlands. Seasonal flamingo concentrations and resident rhino. Lakeshore dawn watches and escarpment raptor scanning.
2
Nakuru → Naivasha
Freshwater lake with papyrus fringes, hippos, fish eagles and shoreline species. Boat trip and Crescent Island walk.
3
Fly to Entebbe & Transfer to Murchison Area
A cross-border flight and scenic transfer into Uganda's savanna and Nile corridor.
4
Murchison Falls Game Drive
Open savanna and riverine forest mosaic hold elephants, lions, giraffe and buffalo. Afternoon game drive and sunset views over the falls.
5
Boat Trip to the Base of the Falls
A Nile boat cruise with dense hippo and crocodile sightings and rich lakeshore birdlife, ending near the thundering base of the falls.
6
Additional Drives or Budongo Chimp Optional
A choice between extra game drives or an optional visit to Budongo Forest for chimpanzee trekking.
7
Transfer to Kampala/Entebbe with Stops
A scenic return journey with cultural stops en route back toward Entebbe.
8
Entebbe Leisure or Birding at the Botanical Gardens
Relaxed birding near the lakeshore and botanical gardens to close out the trip.
9
Departure from Entebbe
Final transfer and international departure.
