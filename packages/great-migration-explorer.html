Great Migration Explorer

A migration-focused sprint across the Mara–Serengeti ecosystem, timed and routed for the best chance of dramatic river crossings and predator action.

Wildebeest River CrossingsNorthern Serengeti HerdsCentral Serengeti PredatorsRemote Fly-in Camps

Itinerary

1
Nairobi → Masai Mara
Classic Mara plains and riparian strips, with plains herbivores building into larger herds in migration season. Orientation and evening drive to stake out likely crossing points.
2
Mara Full Day (Migration Focus)
Riverine and floodplain mosaics may hold dramatic wildebeest crossings in season, with crocodiles and vulture congregations at kills. Full-day drives timed with crossing predictions.
3
Cross into Northern Serengeti
Remote northern plains with sweeping vistas hold massive moving herds in season and large predator coalitions. Remote-camp or fly-in access minimises transit time.
4
Northern Serengeti Full Day
Open grassland punctuated by riverine channels and kopjes hosts migration herds and concentrated predator activity. Long-range drives follow herd movements and crossing behaviour.
5
Move to Central Serengeti
Rolling plains with rocky kopjes ideal for predators, with regular carcass sites and abundant resident game. Hidden photographic hides and kopje-based observation points.
6
Central Serengeti Full Day
Classic plains with photo-friendly light and open sightlines hold lions, cheetahs and frequent hyena activity. Dawn-to-dusk drives focused on action sequences.
7
Wind Down & Transfer to Arusha
Transitional highlands and farmland en route. Final morning drive then a scenic transfer toward Arusha for evening relaxation.
8
Departure from Arusha/Kilimanjaro
Airport transfer and time to consolidate images and field notes before departure.
