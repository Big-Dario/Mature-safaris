Birding & Lakes Specialist

A specialist circuit for birders — flamingo-packed Rift Valley lakes in Kenya, a shoebill canoe search at Mabamba Swamp, and the waterbird-rich Kazinga Channel in Uganda.

Lake Nakuru FlamingosMabamba Shoebill Canoe TripKazinga Channel Boat SafariOptional Kyambura Gorge

Itinerary

1
Nairobi → Lake Nakuru
Alkaline lake with reedbeds hosting flamingo aggregations and diverse waterbirds. Lakeshore dawn watches and checklist assembly.
2
Nakuru → Baringo/Naivasha
Freshwater and alkaline systems with papyrus fringes shelter papyrus specialists, waterbirds and hippos. Boat trips and papyrus-edge searches.
3
Early Baringo Boat → Nairobi → Flight to Entebbe
An early-morning boat outing followed by a cross-border flight into Uganda.
4
Mabamba Swamp — Shoebill Canoe Trip
Papyrus channels and swamp habitat targeted for the elusive shoebill and other papyrus specialists via a quiet canoe trip.
5
Mabamba → Queen Elizabeth Transfer
An evening arrival at Queen Elizabeth National Park in preparation for the Kazinga Channel boat trip.
6
Kazinga Channel Boat & Game Drive
Dense hippo pods, crocodiles and abundant waterbird sightings along one of Africa's richest wildlife channels.
7
Kyambura Gorge Optional Chimp and Birding
An optional gorge walk for primates and riverine bird species in the "Valley of the Apes".
8
Return to Entebbe Leisure and Gardens
A final round of birding around the lakeshore and botanical gardens.
9
Departure
Airport transfer to conclude the birding and lakes specialist circuit.
