Amboseli to Kilimanjaro Safari

Elephant herds beneath Kilimanjaro in Kenya's Amboseli, then across the border to Tanzania's Lake Manyara and the baobab-studded plains of Tarangire.

Amboseli Elephants & KilimanjaroLake Manyara Tree-Climbing LionsTarangire BaobabsBig Elephant Herds

Itinerary

1
Nairobi → Amboseli
Short-grass plains and seasonal marshes with a panoramic Kilimanjaro backdrop. Iconic elephant herds, buffalo and plains groups. Sunset photography of elephants against Kilimanjaro.
2
Amboseli Full Day
Marsh-fed wetlands and open plains showcase elephant family dynamics, hippos, lions and cheetah activity. All-day drives with a Maasai community cultural experience.
3
Amboseli → Lake Manyara Area
A gradual shift to woodland and lake-edge habitat of the Rift floor, crossing into Tanzania. Evening lakeside drive and checklist consolidation.
4
Lake Manyara Full Day
Groundwater forest, alkaline lake and rift cliffs hold possible tree-climbing lions, seasonal flamingo flocks and diverse forest-edge primates. Forest and lake-edge drives.
5
Tarangire Full Day
Baobab-dotted savanna and seasonal floodplain host massive elephant congregations, resident predators and abundant plains ungulates. Sunset drives among dramatic baobabs.
6
Transfer to Arusha and Departure
Final morning game drive before the airport transfer for outbound flights.
