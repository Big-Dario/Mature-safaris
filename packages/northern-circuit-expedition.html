Northern Circuit Expedition

A grand twelve-day sweep from Samburu's northern specialists and Ol Pejeta's rhinos, through the Masai Mara, into the Serengeti, Ngorongoro Crater and Tarangire.

Samburu Northern SpecialistsOl Pejeta Rhino SanctuaryMasai Mara & Serengeti MigrationNgorongoro CraterTarangire Elephants

Itinerary

1
Nairobi → Samburu
Semi-arid acacia scrub, riverine gallery forest and rocky kopjes. Northern specials such as Grevy's zebra, gerenuk and reticulated giraffe at the Ewaso Ng'iro. Evening river transect.
2
Samburu Full Day
Thornbush and riverine mosaics hold unique northern species and predators using river edges as hunting grounds. Morning and late-afternoon specialist drives.
3
Samburu → Laikipia/Ol Pejeta
Conservancy country with river channels, ranchlands and small wetlands. Rhinos at Ol Pejeta, elephants, lions and diverse birdlife. Conservancy walks and rhino sanctuary visit.
4
Laikipia Full Day
Open plains, riverine belts and private conservancies with potential wild dog sightings and abundant raptors. Guided conservancy drives and photographic hides.
5
Laikipia → Masai Mara
A shift to Mara savanna and river corridors, with a high density of predators and large herbivore aggregations upon arrival. Evening drive and camp briefing.
6
Masai Mara Full Day
Dawn and dusk drives focus on predator behaviour and migration activity where present across the Mara's plains and riverine corridors.
7
Masai Mara Full Day
Continued intensive game drives, with an optional balloon safari and community visits available.
8
Mara → Serengeti Cross-Border Transfer
Northern Serengeti's remote plains hold herd movement corridors and predator tracking sites. Afternoon remote-drive in quieter Serengeti zones.
9
Serengeti Full Day
Full-day drives across kopje country with prolonged predator observation and abundant resident game.
10
Serengeti → Ngorongoro Highlands
A climb into the highlands with a crater-rim sunset stop en route.
11
Ngorongoro Crater Drive
Dense crater-floor biodiversity offers excellent black rhino chances, abundant grazers and a concentration of predators. Full-day crater descent with picnic lunch.
12
Tarangire Half-Day → Arusha Departure
A morning drive among Tarangire's baobabs and elephant herds, then transfer to Arusha for departure.
