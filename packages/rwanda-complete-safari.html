Rwanda · 7 Days

Rwanda Complete Safari

Rwanda in full — mountain gorillas and golden monkeys in Volcanoes National Park, chimpanzee trekking in ancient Nyungwe Forest, and a Big Five finale at Akagera.

Mountain Gorilla TrekkingNyungwe Canopy Walk & ChimpsAkagera Big FiveLake Kivu Boat Cruise

Itinerary

1
Kigali → Volcanoes National Park
Transfer north to the Virunga foothills, with an afternoon city stop at the Kigali Genocide Memorial en route for historical context.
2
Mountain Gorilla Trekking
A full-day permit-led trek to spend an unforgettable hour observing a mountain gorilla family in dense montane forest.
3
Volcanoes → Nyungwe Forest
A scenic drive south through Rwanda's hills to ancient Nyungwe Forest, one of Africa's oldest rainforests. Evening briefing on the following day's canopy walk.
4
Nyungwe Canopy Walk & Chimpanzee Trekking
A dawn chimpanzee trek through the forest, followed by a walk across the suspended canopy walkway high above the treetops.
5
Nyungwe → Akagera National Park
A long transfer east to Akagera, Rwanda's savanna reserve on the Tanzanian border, arriving in time for an evening game drive.
6
Akagera Full Day
Full-day game drives and a boat safari on Lake Ihema in search of the Big Five, hippos and crocodiles amid classic East African savanna.
7
Akagera → Kigali Departure
A final morning game drive before the transfer back to Kigali for international departure.
