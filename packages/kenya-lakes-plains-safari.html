Kenya Lakes & Plains Safari

A rarely-visited circuit through the steaming hot springs of Lake Bogoria, the boat channels of Lake Baringo, and on to Nakuru and the Masai Mara.

Lake Bogoria Hot SpringsLake Baringo Boat CruiseLake NakuruMasai Mara Migration

Itinerary

1
Nairobi → Lake Bogoria
An alkaline lake with hot springs and geysers, ringed by acacia scrub. Seasonal flamingo concentrations and varied waders. Dawn and dusk flamingo watches beside steaming springs.
2
Bogoria → Baringo
Freshwater lakes with papyrus fringes and riverine woodland. Kingfishers, herons and a mix of waterbirds, with hippos sheltering in quiet bays.
3
Baringo Full Day
Guided boat trips and island walks explore Baringo's freshwater habitats and their resident birdlife up close.
4
Transfer to Nakuru
Lakeshore exploration and escarpment viewpoints en route, taking in flamingo flocks and raptor activity around Nakuru.
5
Nakuru → Masai Mara
Transfer south into the Mara ecosystem, with an introductory evening game drive upon arrival.
6
Masai Mara Full Day
Extended drives with a migration focus when in season, tracking the Mara's resident predators and herbivore herds.
7
Masai Mara Full Day
A further full day of Mara exploration, rounding out the checklist of plains and predator species.
8
Return to Nairobi
Final morning game drive before the transfer back to Nairobi, concluding a rarely-travelled lakes-and-plains circuit.
