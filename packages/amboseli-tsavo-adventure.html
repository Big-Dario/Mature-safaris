Amboseli & Tsavo Adventure

Kilimanjaro views over Amboseli's elephant herds, then south into Tsavo's volcanic wilderness — lava flows, red-dust elephants and the crystal springs of Mzima.

Amboseli ElephantsMzima SpringsShetani Lava FlowsTsavo Red-Dust ElephantsChyulu Hills Views

Itinerary

1
Nairobi → Amboseli
Open short-grass plains and seasonal marshes beneath Kilimanjaro. Elephants dominate the scene alongside healthy zebra, wildebeest and predator populations. Evening photographic session at dusk.
2
Amboseli Full Day
Marsh-fed floodplains showcase elephant herding behaviour, hippos, and active lion and cheetah at prime hours. Long drives for behaviour observation.
3
Amboseli → Tsavo West
Volcanic ridges, dense scrub and riverine patches. Mzima Springs hosts hippos and crocodiles in clear water; buffalo and elephant frequent the wooded valleys en route.
4
Tsavo West Full Day
Rugged volcanic terrain, lava flows and mixed woodland conceal leopards in dense thickets alongside elephant herds. Scenic drives to the Shetani lava flows.
5
Tsavo East Full Day
Wide red-soil plains scattered with acacia and thorn scrub — home to Tsavo's iconic red-dust elephants, large ungulate herds and resident predators.
6
Transfer to Mombasa or Nairobi
A final morning game drive before transferring onward to the coast at Mombasa, or back to Nairobi for departure.
