Tanzania Serengeti Explorer

Explore the legendary Serengeti and the world-famous Ngorongoro Crater — home to the greatest wildlife concentration on Earth.

Serengeti PlainsNgorongoro CraterWildebeest MigrationOlduvai Gorge

Itinerary

1
Arusha → Serengeti
Fly or drive to Serengeti. Afternoon game drive in the Central Serengeti.
2
Serengeti Full Day
Full day exploring. Seek the Big Five on the vast golden plains.
3
Serengeti → Ngorongoro
Morning drive then move to the Ngorongoro Conservation Area. Crater rim dinner.
4
Ngorongoro Crater
Full day descent into the crater — the world's largest intact caldera, teeming with wildlife.
5
Return to Arusha
Morning at leisure before return transfer to Arusha airport.
