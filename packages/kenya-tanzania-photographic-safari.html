Kenya–Tanzania Photographic Safari

Built for photographers — golden-hour drives, guided hides and vantage points across the Mara, Serengeti, Ngorongoro Crater and Lake Manyara, with a dedicated portfolio-review day.

Golden Hour Game DrivesGuided Photo HidesOptional Balloon Aerial ShotsPortfolio Review Day

Itinerary

1
Mara Arrival & Orientation
Open savanna light and kopje silhouettes set the stage for initial big-cat and plains-herd framing. Composition and lighting orientation with a field talk on technique.
2-4
Mara & Move into Serengeti
Plains, floodplains and kopjes form an excellent photographic stage for high-action predator sequences and seasonal migration scenes. Drives timed for golden hour, optional balloon.
5
Central Serengeti Photography Day
Use of hides and vantage points for action and portrait shots of predators and elephants across classic Serengeti terrain.
6
Serengeti → Ngorongoro Rim Transfer
Crater-rim sunset panoramas offer prime landscape photography as the group ascends into the highlands.
7
Ngorongoro Crater Photography Day
Close-range wildlife portraiture in the crater amphitheatre, home to dense concentrations of grazers and predators.
8
Lake Manyara Dawn Shoots
Forest edge and alkaline lake reflective surfaces offer reflection shots of seasonal flamingos and forest-mammal portraits.
9
Portfolio Consolidation & Re-shoots
Field review, targeted re-shoots and composition feedback to round out the trip's image collection.
10
Departure via Arusha/Kilimanjaro
Final transfers and image-backup advice before international departure.
