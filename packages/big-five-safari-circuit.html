Big Five Safari Circuit

A focused two-country pursuit of the Big Five — Ol Pejeta's rhino sanctuary, the Masai Mara's predators, and a Serengeti-to-Ngorongoro finale.

Ol Pejeta Rhino SanctuaryMasai Mara Big FiveSerengeti PredatorsNgorongoro Crater Rhino

Itinerary

1
Laikipia Arrival (Ol Pejeta)
Private conservancy with river channels and open plains offers a high rhino encounter probability, elephants, and healthy carnivore presence. Rhino sanctuary visit.
2
Laikipia Full Day
Conservancy walks and waterhole hides provide photographic opportunities among the conservancy's resident wildlife.
3
Laikipia → Masai Mara Transfer
Transfer south to the Mara, with an evening game drive upon arrival to begin the Big Five search in earnest.
4-5
Masai Mara Full Days
Iconic plains and riverine belts hold lions, leopards, cheetahs, buffalo and elephants — the focus is squarely on Big Five occurrence. All-day predator-focused drives.
6
Mara → Serengeti Cross-Border
Afternoon Serengeti drive in remote areas continues the Big Five pursuit across the border into Tanzania.
7
Serengeti Full Day
Plains drives and kopje exploration for predator observation across the Serengeti's classic terrain.
8
Serengeti → Ngorongoro Rim Transfer
Crater-rim viewpoints and sunset mark the ascent into the highlands ahead of the final crater descent.
9
Ngorongoro Crater Drive & Departure
An intensive crater-floor game drive targets any remaining Big Five sightings before the transfer to Arusha.
