Zanzibar Spice & Beach Retreat

Explore the exotic spice island — ancient Stone Town, pristine coral beaches, dolphin watching and traditional dhow sunset cruises.

Stone Town UNESCO SiteSpice Farm TourDolphin WatchingSnorkellingSunset Dhow Cruise

Itinerary

1
Arrival in Zanzibar
Airport transfer to beach resort. Afternoon at leisure on Nungwi or Paje beach.
2
Stone Town & Spice Tour
Explore UNESCO World Heritage Stone Town. Afternoon spice farm tour.
3
Dolphin Watching
Morning boat trip to swim with wild dolphins at Kizimkazi. Afternoon snorkelling at coral reefs.
4
Beach Day & Dhow Cruise
Relaxing beach day followed by a traditional sunset dhow cruise with fresh seafood.
5
Departure
Final morning swim before airport transfer.
