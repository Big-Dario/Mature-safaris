Southern Highlights

Kenya's volcanic Tsavo wilderness and Kilimanjaro-backed Amboseli, followed by a crossing into Tanzania's baobab-studded Tarangire.

Mzima SpringsTsavo Red-Dust ElephantsAmboseli & KilimanjaroTarangire Baobabs

Itinerary

1
Nairobi → Tsavo West
Volcanic ridges, Shetani lava flows and riverine springs at Mzima. Hippos and crocodiles at the springs, robust elephant and buffalo numbers. Visit to Mzima Springs pools.
2
Tsavo West Full Day
Mixed woodland and lava-flow terrain conceal leopards in dense thickets alongside elephant herds. Scenic drives and sunset photography across lava features.
3
Tsavo West → Tsavo East Transfer
A move to red-dust plains and expansive open country, home to iconic red-dust elephants, large plains herds and resident predators. Wide-ranging drives with picnic lunches.
4
Tsavo East → Amboseli Transfer
Transition from red-soil plains to Kilimanjaro-backed Amboseli marshes, with elephants and marsh-edge species. Sunset Kilimanjaro silhouette photography.
5
Amboseli Full Day
Full-day drives around marshes and short-grass plains, following elephant herds and resident predators.
6
Amboseli → Tarangire (Tanzania)
Baobab-studded Tarangire landscape with seasonal wetlands holds huge elephant concentrations, diverse ungulates and predators. Late-afternoon drives among dramatic baobabs.
7
Tarangire → Arusha and Departure
A final morning drive before the transfer for outbound flights.
