Kenya Big Five Trail

A focused pursuit of Africa's Big Five — rhino sanctuaries at Ol Pejeta, flamingo-lined Nakuru, three days in the predator-rich Mara, and a Kilimanjaro finale at Amboseli.

Ol Pejeta Rhino SanctuaryLake NakuruExtended Masai MaraAmboseli Elephants & Kilimanjaro

Itinerary

1
Nairobi → Laikipia/Ol Pejeta
Conservancy ridges and riverine woodland offer a high probability of rhino sightings at Ol Pejeta, plus diverse carnivores and elephant herds.
2
Laikipia Full Day
Conservancy activities including guided walks and waterhole hides deepen the search for the Big Five across private-land habitat.
3
Transfer to Lake Nakuru
Lakeshore birding and raptor vantage points along the alkaline lake, with resident rhino and buffalo within park boundaries.
4
Nakuru → Masai Mara
Drive to the Mara ecosystem with an evening introductory game drive to orient within predator-rich savanna.
5
Masai Mara Intensive Drives
The first of two full days of multi-area Mara exploration, tracking lion, leopard, buffalo and elephant across riverine belts and kopjes.
6
Masai Mara Intensive Drives
A second full day of predator tracking and Big Five checklist drives across the reserve's varied habitat.
7
Transfer to Amboseli
Afternoon arrival into Amboseli, with elephant sightings set against classic Kilimanjaro views.
8
Amboseli Full Day
A full day of Amboseli drives and photography, focused on elephant herding behaviour and the mountain backdrop.
9
Return to Nairobi
Final morning game drive before the transfer back to Nairobi, closing out the Big Five trail.
