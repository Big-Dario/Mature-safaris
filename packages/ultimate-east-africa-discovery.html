Ultimate East Africa Discovery

The most complete East Africa itinerary on offer — highland Aberdares, Samburu's north, the Masai Mara, Serengeti, Ngorongoro Crater and Tarangire, in one twelve-day grand tour.

Aberdares Highland ForestSamburu Northern SpecialistsExtended Masai MaraSerengeti & NgorongoroTarangire Baobabs

Itinerary

1
Nairobi → Aberdares
Highland forest, moorland and waterhole platforms hold highland-specialist mammals and montane birdlife. Evening waterhole platform sessions and forest walks.
2
Aberdares Full Day
Forest walks, ridge scans and moorland exploration in search of the range's montane species.
3
Aberdares → Samburu Transfer
A descent into semi-arid Ewaso Ng'iro country rich in adaptive northern species. Afternoon riverbank drive and sunset camp arrival.
4
Samburu Full Day
Targeted drives for regional specialists and riverine wildlife across Samburu's thornbush and gallery-forest habitat.
5
Samburu → Masai Mara
A drive south to the Mara with en-route stops and an evening game drive on arrival.
6-7
Masai Mara Extended Drives
Two full days of predator tracking and migration observation where seasonal, with an optional balloon safari over the plains.
8
Mara → Serengeti Cross-Border
A remote Serengeti arrival and late-afternoon drive across the border into Tanzania's vast plains.
9
Serengeti Full Day
Kopje and plains exploration with sustained game-viewing across the Serengeti's classic predator country.
10
Serengeti → Ngorongoro Highlands
An ascent to the highlands with a crater-rim viewpoint at sunset.
11
Ngorongoro Crater Drive
A whole-day crater drive with picnic lunch, focused on rhino sightings and dense herbivore packs within the caldera.
12
Tarangire Half-Day → Arusha Departure
A morning Tarangire drive among baobabs and elephant herds, then a final transfer to Arusha/Kilimanjaro for departure.
