Southern Kenya Highlights

Amboseli's elephants, Tsavo's volcanic wilderness, and a final stretch to Kenya's coast at Mombasa or Diani — savanna and shoreline in one journey.

Amboseli & KilimanjaroMzima SpringsTsavo Red-Dust ElephantsCoastal Finale

Itinerary

1
Nairobi → Amboseli
Afternoon drives target elephant sightings with Kilimanjaro as a backdrop, ideal for the trip's signature photography.
2
Amboseli Full Day
Full-day game drives across marsh and plain habitats within Amboseli, following elephant families and resident predators.
3
Transfer to Tsavo West (Mzima Springs)
Volcanic landscapes, springs and riverine woodland lead to Mzima Springs, where hippos and clear-water wildlife concentrate.
4
Tsavo West Drives
Scenic drives through lava flows and scrub habitat in search of leopard, elephant and Tsavo's varied savanna species.
5
Tsavo East Exploration
Wide-ranging drives across Tsavo East's open plains for the park's iconic red-dust elephants and abundant herbivore herds.
6
Transfer Toward Mombasa / Diani
A coastal transfer trades savanna for shoreline, with time to relax at the beach on arrival.
7
Departure from Mombasa
Optional marine activities or a return transfer to Nairobi to conclude the highland-to-coast journey.
