Uganda Gorilla Trek

Track endangered mountain gorillas in the misty forests of Bwindi — one of the most profound wildlife encounters on Earth.

Mountain Gorilla TrekkingBwindi ForestChimpanzee TrackingLocal Culture

Itinerary

1
Entebbe → Bwindi
Fly or drive to Bwindi Impenetrable National Park. Briefing and overnight at forest lodge.
2
Gorilla Trekking
The experience of a lifetime. 1 hour with a habituated gorilla family in their natural habitat.
3
Chimp Tracking (Optional)
Optional chimpanzee tracking in Kibale Forest or cultural visit to a local community.
4
Return to Entebbe
Morning at leisure. Return transfer to Entebbe.
