Lakes & Plains Adventure

Kenya's Rift Valley lakes — Naivasha and Nakuru — flow into the Masai Mara, then across the border into the Serengeti and Ngorongoro Crater.

Lake Naivasha Boat RideLake Nakuru FlamingosMasai Mara MigrationNgorongoro Crater

Itinerary

1
Nairobi → Lake Naivasha
Freshwater lake with papyrus fringes and riparian woodland, home to hippos, African fish eagle and abundant waterbird life. Afternoon boat trip and Crescent Island walk.
2
Naivasha → Lake Nakuru
Alkaline lake environment with short grass margins and escarpment viewpoints. Seasonal flamingos, pelicans and resident rhino and buffalo. Lakeshore scanning at sunrise.
3
Lake Nakuru Full Day
Expanded lakeshore, reedbeds and woodland pockets offer good rhino observations and waterbird diversity. Full-day lake circuit with hides and photo stops.
4
Nakuru → Masai Mara
Transition from Rift Valley to extensive savanna and riverine mosaics of the Mara, with plains herds and predator concentrations upon arrival.
5
Masai Mara Full Day
Broad plains, kopjes and riverine corridors hold big cats, hyenas and abundant herbivores, with seasonal migration spectacle. Optional cultural visit or balloon departure.
6
Mara → Serengeti (Cross-Border)
The Mara ecosystem flows into Serengeti plains and kopjes, with continuity of migration and resident predators. Scenic cross-border transfer and afternoon Serengeti drive.
7
Serengeti Full Day
Wide-open savanna with isolated kopjes hosts lions, cheetahs, hyenas and large herbivore aggregations. Early-morning kopje vantage and late-afternoon plains drives.
8
Serengeti → Ngorongoro Highlands
An ascent to montane grassland and crater-rim vistas, with transitional species and raptor watching from the rim, ending at a sunset viewpoint.
9
Ngorongoro Crater Drive & Transfer to Arusha
The crater floor holds high densities of mammals including black rhino, buffalo and predators. Full-day crater drive with picnic, then late transfer to Arusha.
